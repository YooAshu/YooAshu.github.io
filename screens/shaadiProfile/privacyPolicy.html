Privacy Policy

Shaadi Profile respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Shaadi Profile mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

Shaadi Profile does not require account registration. Any personal information you enter — such as name, date of birth, contact details, education, occupation, family information, religion, caste, or photos — is stored locally on your device only. This information is never uploaded to any server and is not accessible to us.

2. Sensitive Personal Data

The App allows users to optionally enter sensitive details such as Religion and Caste for the purpose of generating a biodata document. We strictly DO NOT share, sell, transmit, or use this sensitive data in any way beyond local document generation on your device. This data is never linked to advertising, analytics, or any third-party service.

3. Photos and Storage Access

The App may request access to your device's photo library or storage only when you choose to add a profile photo or save a generated biodata file. This access is used solely for the stated functionality and no images are transmitted outside your device.

On Android 13 and above, the App requests READ_MEDIA_IMAGES permission. On older versions, it requests READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE. Storage write access is requested only when saving generated biodata files.

4. No Advertisements

Shaadi Profile is currently an ad-free application. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs, and no user data is shared with advertisers or marketing firms.

5. Premium Purchase

Users may purchase a one-time premium upgrade through Google Play Billing to unlock all premium templates and enable professional features. All payments are processed securely by Google Play. We do not collect, store, or have access to any payment or billing information. Purchases are subject to Google Play's terms of service.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data with any third party. Third-party services integrated in the App — namely Google Play Billing — operate under their own independent privacy policies. No personal biodata content is shared with these services.

7. Data Storage and Security

All biodata and profile information is stored locally on your device. You have full control over this data, including deletion by uninstalling the App or clearing App data from your device settings. We do not maintain any remote servers or databases containing your personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

Shaadi Profile is intended for use by adults for the purpose of creating marriage biodata. The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the App or applicable regulations. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
