Privacy Policy

Shaadi Profile respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Shaadi Profile mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Shaadi Profile does not require account registration. Any personal information you enter (such as name, contact details, photos, or family information) is stored locally on your device only.

2. Use of Personal Data

Personal data entered in the app is used solely to create and manage biodata. This information is not uploaded to any server and is not accessible to us.

3. Photos and Storage

The app may request access to photos or storage only when you choose to add images or save generated biodata files. This access is used strictly for app functionality.

4. Advertisements

Shaadi Profile displays advertisements using third-party services such as Google AdMob. These services may collect limited, non-personal information (such as device identifiers) to show relevant ads.

5. Rewarded Ads

Some premium templates or features may be accessed temporarily by watching a rewarded advertisement. Watching ads is optional and requires user consent.

6. Premium Purchase

Users may purchase a one-time premium upgrade to remove ads and unlock premium features. All payments are processed securely through Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store payment information.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data. Third-party services used by the app operate under their own privacy policies.

8. Data Security

Since data is stored locally on your device, you control its availability and deletion. We encourage users to protect their devices with appropriate security measures.

9. Children’s Privacy

Shaadi Profile is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

11. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app’s Google Play Store listing.
